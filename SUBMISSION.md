## 60-90 Second Walkthrough Script

Hi, I am Jayanth. I saw the fast-track option for the SignalsHQ frontend intern role and wanted to show execution instead of only sending a resume.

SignalsHQ is solving a serious workflow problem for CPA firms, so I did not want to make a generic landing page. I redesigned the page around the actual user journey: client documents come in, AI extracts key information, tax research gets answered with context, and reviewers see risks before sign-off.

The hero shows a product preview immediately, because buyers should understand the product before they scroll. The workflow section is interactive, with intake, research, and review states. I also added a simulated AI tax assistant to show how an AI-native interface can move from a question to a documented answer and next action.

I kept the visual style restrained because this is financial data. The UI should feel trustworthy, fast, and operational, not decorative.

I used React and AI-assisted development tools to move quickly, but the product decisions, information architecture, and UX choices were intentional. I would love to bring this same ownership and speed to the internship.
